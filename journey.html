My Journey

This page tracks my long-term development as a student, athlete, and builder. My goal is to improve steadily with clear plans, consistent effort, and measurable progress.

Instead of chasing quick results, I focus on habits I can repeat daily. This approach helps me stay consistent, improve under pressure, and keep moving forward even when progress feels slow.

Basketball Development

Academics and Learning

I spend time revising concepts, improving learning methods, and reflecting on what works best. This helps me perform better while keeping the learning process meaningful.

Current target: Log milestone achievements and learning wins each term.
